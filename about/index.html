About Us

What is Drextab?

Drextab is a customised search platform that allows you to search from a plethora of websites with included customisation and anonymity. We basically take in your query and redirect you to your desired engine so that it cannot track information like browser, search source, etc.

Why Drextab?

Unlike regular search engines, Drextab doesn't actually search for websites but rather redirects your queries to them. Which means you can search faster and with all the beautiful themes that you want! Drextab also makes sure to neither collect nor let search engines collect your private data while typing. This includes keylogging and sensitive browser fingerprinting.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide users with an aesthetic environment to solve your queries. You should not open your browser to see a dull and ugly welcome page. Instead you can use our completely free service and show it off to your friends!

Our Logo

Our logo, "The Pinnaccle Sphere" is an abstract design which perfectly encompasses our main principle - anonymity. The spherical shape represents our vision of being used all aroud the globe.
